The importance of the bones

Bones are really important because they don't only help for protection or movement, they also help by storing and releasing minerals to help the body.

The skeletal system

The skeletal system is all the bones on our body where we can see what they do and how they work. We can aslo se what they are made of.

Number of bones in our body

The human adult body has 206 bones.

Joints and bone connections

A joint is a place where bones conect to each other. Depending on its movement it's classified as some type of joint. Immovable joints that allows no movement at all. Slightly movable joints permit a small amount of restricted movement and freely movable joints let you move in one or more directions.
