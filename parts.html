Compact Bone

Compact bone is a hard and dense part of the bone, it is the outer layer, and protects the inside of the bone.

Spongy Bone

The spongy bone is lightweight and it has holes, it is found in the inner bone. It helps contain spaces where bone marrow may be found and it helps absorb hits and reduce bone weight.

Bone Marrow

Bone marrow is a soft tissue found inside the bone there is two types of bone marrows, red and yellow.

Red and Yellow Bone Marrow

The red bone marrow is responsible for blood cell production and the yellow bone marrow is responsible for fat storage to release when needing energy.
