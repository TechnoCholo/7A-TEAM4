Bone functions

Support

The bones give the body its shape and helps us stand up straight to not wobble or fall that easily.

Movement

Bones and muscles work together for this function to let us jump, run and generally move.

Protection

Bones in this function protect vital/important organs like the brain, lungs and spinal cord. These work by being around the organs.
