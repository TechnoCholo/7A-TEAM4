Long bones

Long bones are hard and dense, they provide structure, mobility and stability, these bones act as levers to pull muscles. A example of a long bone could be the Femur.

Short bones

Short bones have a cube like form that almost all of them have the same width and strenght. They provide stablity and limited movement.

Flat bones

These bones are flat and curved. They primarly work to protect vital internal organs and provide a surface for muscles to attach.

Irregular bones

These bones have a complex shape making them not fit on other categories. Becuase of their shape they have specialized tasks.
